Assignment 3

Choose one of the maps that you have developed in the HARD SYSTEM or SOFT SYSTEM assignments.

Based on our comments, your growing understanding of urban systems and coupled with your knowledge and technical expertise from previous assignments complete the following minimum requirements;

  • Focus on visualizing the agents and flows in the map - try to find ways to represent different agents, their quantities, and directions.
  • Add another layer(s) that is based on your analysis of the different components of your system. This should be a visual representation of your thoughts and observations from the data.
  • Develop a novel conclusion based on the above analysis

Deliverable - Please submit a 2-minute video with a voiceover that explains your added layer.
