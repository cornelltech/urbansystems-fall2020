Assignment 2

Identify, analyze, and represent a SOFT SYSTEM

Deliverable - 2 minutes screenshot video of the map, with a description of your intent and observations.

  1. Choose a SOFT SYSTEM in the city of New York
  2. Identify the different elements of this system: Nodes, Agents, Flows, Network
  3. Define who and what are the agents and what are their attributes
  4. Create an interactive map that shows
    • the network structure
    • identifies the nodes
    • quantifies the flows
  5. Represent the locations and connections between those elements

Leading questions for the assignment

  • What are the differences between a hard and a soft system?
  • How will these differences affect your analysis?
  • Does a soft system have a purpose, what is it made for or what is its aim?
  • Can you identify the parts that compose this system ?
  • How are those parts connected (spatially, logically, chronologically, hierarchically) ?
  • How do they interact with each other?
  • When and how is the system must efficient? or are there ways to measure success in a soft system?
  • What are the bottle necks/flaws/breakdown points, of the system?
