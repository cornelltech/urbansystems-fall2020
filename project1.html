Project 1

Restarting the economy

Miro board: Project 1 Presentations

Reopening Offices, Construction and Manufacturing Centers

You are strategic planning team for the Chief Technology Officer for the city’s Economic Development Corporation. To help jump start the local economy you are tasked with developing a strategy to open the city’s workplaces; specifically offices, construction sites and manufacturing centers.

What are the challenges and constraints of reopening these facilities safely in order to help support the city’s cratering economy?

Using the tools of urban technology develop an approach to safely open these facilities. Tools can include data analytics, novel approaches to mobility, use of AI, IoT technologies, ICT, automation and computer vision. Part of this challenge will include a comprehensive plan that includes accommodating all types of workers from across the city.

You will be graded on the creativity and comprehensiveness of your approach and your novel use of the tools of urban technology to support the reopening of this system. Additional credit will be given for ideas on how to create a more resilient urban system of workplaces

As part of this project, you will develop a group presentation that includes the following minimum requirements;

  1. Identify the impacts of the closures on the economy including other urban systems affected by the closures
  2. What are the challenges and opportunities for safely reopening Office, Construction sites and/or manufacturing centers?
  3. Identify Goals of the Reopening.
  4. Define Metrics of Success.

  6. Techniques and proposals for connecting housing and jobs.
  7. Strategies for de-densifying workplaces while building a creative workplace, scheduling, layout, WFM practices (if applicable), etc..
